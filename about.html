about me

hello! my name’s syd nguyen (sid nuh-when), and I’m a second-year at the University of Virginia, pursuing a degree in data science. My favorite word is "snafu," my favorite sans serif is Montserrat, and my favorite intersection is that which exists between data, computing, and the humanities (though the one between linguistics and stenography is also cool).


I’m a designer, programmer, writer, musician, and above all — a creative. I’m currently a design marketing intern at TextP2P, but I’m looking for more work in data journalism and visualization. this website is a place for all things me, so I hope you enjoy and thanks for stopping by :]
